Loaded Potato Skins Recipe

Description

A fun and easy appetiser or side dish to prepare for the next you have friend and family over, or maybe just as your own treat!

Although this recipie will provide an example of what flavours and fillings to use for these potato skins, the beauty of the recipe is that it is infitely customisable to your own tastes. Potatoes will pair with basially anything and act as a canvas for you to put your own style and inspirations on.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium to large Russet potateos - a non-waxy potato works best!
  • 200 g shredded cheddar cheese.
  • 6 slices of bacon, cooked and diced
  • 8 tsp olive oil
  • Spring oninos, chopped, for topping.
  • Seasoning, salt and ground black pepper, to taste.
